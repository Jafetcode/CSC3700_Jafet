About us at Books R Us

Books R Us is a local book shop that provides both preowned, and new books for purchase!

First Opened

Books R Us first opened in 1946 after Jameson Johnson can back from serving in World War 2. Ever since then, the shop has been operated by close friends and family.

Where are we located?

Right here is good ol' Aurora, IL.

What services do we offer?

We offer an assortment of items! From books, preowned video games and consoles, to even VHS tapes! The list keeps on growing so be on the lookout for what items we might have in store for you next
